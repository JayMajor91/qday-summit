02, About
What is Q-Day?

Q-Day is a curated, single-day gathering at the intersection of quantum computing research and blockchain infrastructure.

Convened by Quantus Network, the world's first quantum-native Layer 1 blockchain, and hosted at Network School in Johor Bahru.
Format

Single-day, invite-only programme with keynotes, panels, and structured networking sessions for 60–80 curated delegates.

Companion Paper

"The State of Quantum Computing: What Crypto Can't Afford to Ignore", pre-distributed to 1,000+ researchers, investors, and press.

Built Quantum-Native

Quantus uses ML-KEM + ML-DSA at consensus, NIST 2024 post-quantum standards. Not migrated. Built from scratch.
